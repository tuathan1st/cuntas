Scan admhála · photograph a receipt

Snap a receipt or invoice. Iompórtáil díolachán · import Etsy / SumUp sales

Download your orders CSV from Etsy (Shop Manager → Settings → Options → Download Data) or your transactions CSV from the SumUp dashboard, then load it here. The app works out which platform it is, creates the income entries in date order, and automatically skips anything already imported — run it as often as you like, you'll never get duplicates.

Cuir isteach · add an entry

Where did you drive? The app remembers each destination's miles — after the first trip somewhere, just pick the place and everything fills itself in. It claims HMRC's approved rate for you (45p/mile for the first 10,000 business miles each tax year, 25p after) — this covers fuel and wear, so don't also claim petrol receipts for the same trips. Look up the one-way miles once on Google Maps; never again after that.

⚡ Connect AI reading (optional)

The app reads receipts on this phone by default — free, private, fully offline. If you want sharper reading (crumpled receipts, screenshots, spotting income vs expense), paste an Anthropic API key from console.anthropic.com and press Save. The app will then use AI when you're online and automatically fall back to the on-device reader when you're not. The key stays on this device only; each scan costs a fraction of a penny. Delete the key any time to disconnect.
